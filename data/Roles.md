### **Assassin**: 
If the Assassin is not kicked off the Island, those who voted for the Assassin can now be assassinated. The Assassin is the only Role that can Kill Someone in Prison. The Assassin has a base chance of killing the target of 25% along with having a 50% chance of surviving an attack. The chances increase if the said target is poisoned or weakened by 12.5%. The Assassin can poison a survivor once per day, this Poison will bring down one’s Health by 1 and Food by 1. Any Survivor on the Island can ask the Assassin to have someone killed. However, this doesn’t go unpunished, the Assassin will need blood as insurance. If the Assassin accepts your proposition, you will lose 2 health points, regardless if the attempt is successful. Having the Machete From Hell will make the base 25% chance to 50% chance. The Same statistics apply. The Assassin’s failed attempts to kill will not be announced. 

##### **Bard**:
The Bard can perform music or storytelling. The bard could either choose to:
- Increase the chances of getting their preferred weather by 50%
- Choose a survivor to harmonically raise morale, making them gain 2 Health points
- Can tell a story to a survivor, can even be false.

#### **Blacksmith**: 
Makes weapons and armor for the Island. The Blacksmith has armor but can also give armor to a survivor to increase their survivability from an attack by 45%. The Blacksmith has to choose whether or not to make the "Machete From Hell" for the [Assassin](Roles#assassin), [Executioner](Roles#Interrogator), and [Nightguard](Roles#nightguard). 

#### **Builder**: 
The Builder is a master craftsman and architect that greatly increase the survivability of the survivors. The Builder will be able to build one Special Build per day. The builds take a day to build so if you request to build on Day 1, your build won't be complete and useable until Day 2.
- Weather Rock:
	- The Bard will 100% dictate what the weather will be and can boost the morale of 2 survivors instead of 1.
- Sacred Jail:
	- This Jail will prevent the survivor from using their Secret Role, along with their ability to vote. This is the only jail that the Assassin cannot break in and out of.
- Taller Nightguard Tower
	- The South area of the Island is fully seen, meaning the Cannibal/Werewolf can only attack from the North, East, and West
- Lecture Hall
	- The Teacher is able to buff 2 people instead of 1. No other part of the Teacher's ability is affected.
- Sol's Bomb Shelter
	- This Bomb Shelter will protect the survivors, except one, from experiencing any negative effects that day. Will also make it so the Cannibal and Werewolf can't kill. However, only 1 can be built.

The Builder's structures will remain on The Island 2 days after the Builder has been kicked off or killed.

#### **Cook**: 
The Cook is able to cook different meals for The Island survivors as long as the [Hunter](Roles#Hunter) or [Fisher](Roles#Fisher) are still on The Island. The Different Meals are: 
-  Gourmet Scorpion Stew:
	- Allows the cook to heal 5 people on the Island for 2 health points and 2 food points. (Must heal 5 people)
- DoorDashed Box Combo, no coleslaw, extra toast butter on both sides, extra crispy chicken and fries with a Large Dr. Pepper lite ice:
	- Takes a day to come through, will allow the consumer to have immunity from being kicked off (not killed). Has a 3 day cooldown, and cannot be used in the Top 3.
- Shura’s 2am Spaghetti:
	- This meal takes a day to take into effect, The energy gained from it allows for an extra increase defense from attacks by 45%.

#### **Farmer**: 
Plants crops for food for The Island. Has the ability to plant a special crop each day. The Special Crops have unique abilities. The Special Crops (Rarity) include:
- Sneaky Spinach (50%)
	- *Makes the consumer immune to nighttime attacks from the Cannibal or Werewolf.*
- Lively Lemons (70%)
	-  *Makes the consumer so energetic they can perform an extra action for the day. The Farmer can only use two Lively Lemons in a day.*
- Tasty Tangerine (70%)
	- *Makes the consumer replenish 3 hearts and 3 hunger points*
- Trippy Tulips (70%)
	- *Makes the consumer trip so hard they are unable to perform their role or vote for the day.*
- Notch Apple (70%)
	-  *Makes the consumer invincible for a day including being kicked off, and also replenishes all health and food. Only 1 is allowed to be grown, it takes 3 days. Rots in the Top 5.*

The Farmer can store 1 Special Crop for a later date. The chances of a successful harvest go down to 50% if the weather is too cold or too hot. The "Old McDonald's Overalls" will give a guaranteed chance of having a successful harvest.

#### **Fisher**: 
Fishes. The Fisher is able to fish out some Special Items from the ocean. The Special Items have unique abilities. The Special Items(Rarity) include: 
- Luminous Seashell (15%)  
	- *Grants the survivor the ability to see the truth behind a survivor's actions for a day, even revealing their secret role if they had any recent activities. This includes who they’re voting for.*
- Gleaming Sardine Scale (15%)
	- *Grants the survivor the ability to wear the Scale as armor reducing attacks by 50%.*
- Moby Dick’s Harpoon (10%)
	- *The Ability to slay any Sea Beast, only 1 is able to be found*
- The Black Pearl (15%)
	- *Ability to summon a Sea Beast for the following. The Same Sea Beast cannot be summoned again.*
- Cursed Cuttlefish Ink (15%)
	- *Makes the survivor blind, unable to perform their role or vote.*

The Fisher is able store 1 Special Item for a later date. Rainy Weather will increase the chances of fishing "Moby Dick's Harpoon" to 20%. "Jeremy Wade's Net" will now make it a guaranteed that one of the Special Items is fished that day. 

#### **Hunter**: 
Hunts for food on the Island. The Hunter has the ability to hunt/tame Special Animals on the Island. The Special animals have unique abilities. The Special Animals(Rarity) include: 
- Ethereal Eagle (15%)
	- *Allows the Hunter to choose who the Eagle watches over for the night and protects them from being attacked by the Cannibal or Werewolf*
- Envious Fox (15%)
	- *Allows the Hunter to give false information about anyone on the Island. Anyone that is using some form of information gathering technique will be given whatever information you make up. For example, the Nightguard may be given the wrong information that they saw the Cannibal and its player “X”.*
- Mystical Owl (15%)
	- *Allow the Hunter to see who everyone is voting for and who will be attacked that day*
- Sif, The Great Grey Wolf (10%)
	- *If the Hunter tames the Wolf, they will have a companion for The Island. The Wolf will protect the Hunter from attacks from The Island, so not just the Cannibal or the Werewolf, but any Land-based Threat, except for the Assassin.*
- Jinxed Jackrabbit (15%)
	- *Allows the Hunter to jinx a player with the inability to perform their role or vote.*

The Hunter will be able store 1 Special Animal for a later date. Rainy weather will negatively affect the chances of a successful hunt by 5%. Clear weather will make the chances of taming "Sif, The Great Grey Wolf" to 20%. "Robin Hood's Hat" will now make it guaranteed that one of the Special Animals is hunted down.

#### **Interrogator**: 
The Interrogator has the ability to interrogate those one the Island to figure out what the player is doing that day. In a successful interrogation the Interrogator will know who they're voting for, what actions they are doing, and if they used their Secret Role the day before. The chances of success are 50%, however a jailed suspect has a 70% chance of telling the truth. The Interrogator becomes an Executioner with the "Machete From Hell". With the "Machete From Hell" the Executioner can execute a survivor that is kicked off. Which will prevent them from being brought back from the dead.

#### **Jailor**: 
Can lock up anyone on the Island. Being locked up prevents one from doing their roles, but also prevents them from being killed by the [Cannibal](Secret-Roles#Cannibal) or the [Werewolf](Secret-Roles#Werewolf). Being in jail doesn’t prevent one from being poisoned or affected by any other negative effects. The [Wizard](Secret-Roles#Wizard) bypasses being locked up and can still cast spells in jail. 

#### **Medic**: 
The Medic has the ability to heal and poison. The Medic's Heal and Poison is 2 Heart Points and 1 Hunger Point. The Medic cannot Heal/Poison the same person twice on the same day. The Medic can also not Poison 2 different people on the same day. The Medic can either Heal/Heal and Heal/Poison.

#### **Nightguard**: 
Chooses a side of the Island to defend. North, East, West, or South. If the Nightguard catches the [Cannibal](Secret-Roles#Cannibal)/[Werewolf](Secret-Roles#Werewolf) in the act of attacking another survivor, he will be able to inform the Island on who the [Cannibal](Secret-Roles#Cannibal)/[Werewolf](Secret-Roles#Werewolf) is. Or lie about it. The Nightguard will have a 50% chance of surviving an attack if they guess the direction, 35% chance if they guess wrong. The Nightguard will also have the ability to wield the Machete From Hell. Which will allow them to defend the survivor who was attacked, assuming that they guessed the right direction, the chances of defending against an attack is 50%. 

#### **Priest**: 
The Priest has the ability to bring back a survivor who has been kicked off and a survivor who has been killed. These are separate abilities, so they can be used at different times or on the same day. Both abilities have a 3 day cooldown. The Priest can also give someone a Cross Necklace/Pendant to prevent one from being affected by any poison and from being killed. The [Wizard](Secret-Roles#Wizard) can not cast spells if the Cross is placed on the [Wizard](Secret-Roles#Wizard) player. The Cross goes away after a day and will have to be given to someone else (or the same person) the next day.

#### **Scavenger**: 
Gets berries, fruits, and nuts from the Island. The Scavenger is able to find Special Artifacts on the Island that are very powerful. The Special Artifacts (Rarity) are: 
- Alternate Timeline (10%)
	- *Allows the Scavenger to enter alternate timelines where events may have played out differently.*
- The Equalizer (10%)
	-  *Allows the Scavenger to Equalize The Island.*
- The Summoning Key (10%)
	- *Allows the Scavenger to summon a Great One.*
- Celium Piece (10%)
	- *Allows the Scavenger to harness the power of one of the Celium Piece's.*

The Scavenger is allowed to hold onto theses and use at a later time except they will be taken away in the Top 4. The "Indiana Jones Satchel" will make the scavenger more likely to find the artifacts by 5%.

#### **Tailor**: 
The Tailor will make clothes for the Island each day. If the wrong clothes are made for the day, everyone will lose a Heart Point. While everyone will gain a Heart Point if the right clothes are worn and there is a structure on the Island. The Tailor also has the ability to make clothes for the Food Roles. The Tailor can craft either “Old McDonald’s Overalls”, “Jeremy Wade’s Net”, “Robin Hoods Hat”, and “Indiana Jones Satchel” for the respective food roles. However, only one of the four can be crafted and given to their respective role, until they are voted off. 

#### **Teacher**: 
Can teach any role, regardless if the role is currently on the Island or not. When someone is taught a role, someone can not be taught again for 3 days. The Teacher buff will increase Heal/Poison by 1 (even affects the [Wizard](Secret-Roles#Wizard)), Increase the Food Roles chances by 10%, Narrow Down the [Nightguard's](Roles#Nighguard) Options to 2, Increase the [Blacksmith’s](Roles#Blacksmith) Armor Protection to 75%, making the [Interrogator’s](Roles#Interrogator) interrogation be 100% successful, Increase the Chances of a successful [Assassination](Roles#Assassin) by adding 12.5%, and for normal roles the buff will decrease the effect of one’s poison by 1. The teacher buff also prevents false information from being given to the survivor with it.
